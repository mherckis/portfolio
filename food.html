Food I Like

Bagels are my favorite food to get at home. I like a plain bagel toasted with veggie cream cheese, lox, and onions.

Over quarantine, it became a hobby of mine to make charcuterie boards: this is one of my favorite masterpieces.

Crispy rice is one of my favorite things to order at a Japanese restaurant. This one had truffle spicy mayo and peppers inside!

One of my favorite desserts is donuts. These are warm, fresh donuts from a place called "The Donut Experiment" in Miami, Florida.

When I'm on the beach, I love a good lobster roll. This is one of my favorites: from a shack on Compo Beach in Westport, CT.

This is the seafood paella from Aventura in Ann Arbor - one of the best paellas I've ever had!

My favorite pasta shape is spaghetti. This dish, from Etta in Chicago, had spiced burrata on top with fresh olive oil and pepper.

Mani Osteria in Ann Arbor makes one of the best margarita pizzas: pictured here!

You can never go wrong with sushi. The roll on the bottom contained spicy mayo, tuna, salmon, and crab salad, topped with crispy wontons.
